The clock is not a metaphor,
it is a machine that breaks.
I have been keeping time
in the old way—
by counting what is lost.

The sun still rises.
I note this with suspicion.
Something that dependable
is either god or trap.

I keep running the numbers.
They do not balance.
I keep running.
